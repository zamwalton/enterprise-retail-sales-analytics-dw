# Purpose

This document defines the key business terms, metrics, and concepts used throughout the Enterprise Retail Sales Analytics Data Warehouse project. It ensures that business users, data engineers, analysts, and BI developers share a common understanding of business terminology.

---

# Business Terms

| Term            | Definition                                                                     |
|-----------------|--------------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| Customer        | An individual or organization that purchases products from the retail company. |
| Product         | An item available for sale through physical or online channels.                |
| Store           | A physical retail location where sales transactions occur.                     |
| Supplier        | A company that provides products to the retailer.                              |
| Promotion       | A marketing campaign offering discounts or incentives to customers.            |
| Inventory       | The quantity of products currently available for sale or storage.              |
| Employee        | A staff member responsible for store operations or sales activities.           |
| Loyalty Program | A customer rewards program that encourages repeat purchases.                   |

---

# Sales Metrics

| Metric                    | Definition                                                            |
|---------------------------|-----------------------------------------------------------------------|
| Sales Amount              | Total revenue generated from a transaction before deducting costs.    |
| Cost Amount               | Cost incurred by the retailer to acquire the sold products.           |
| Profit Amount             | Sales Amount minus Cost Amount.                                       |
| Discount Amount           | Reduction applied to the original sales price as part of a promotion. |
| Quantity Sold             | Number of product units sold in a transaction.                        |
| Average Order Value (AOV) | Total Sales Amount divided by the number of transactions.             |

---

# Customer Metrics

| Metric                        | Definition                                                                                                |
|-------------------------------|-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| Customer Lifetime Value (CLV) | Estimated total revenue generated by a customer over the duration of their relationship with the company. |
| Repeat Customer               | A customer who has completed more than one purchase.                                                      |
| Loyalty Tier                  | Customer classification such as Bronze, Silver, Gold, or Platinum based on business rules.                |

---

# Inventory Metrics

| Metric          | Definition                                                  |
|-----------------|-------------------------------------------------------------|
| Current Stock   | Quantity of items currently available in inventory.         |
| Reserved Stock  | Quantity allocated to pending customer orders.              |
| Available Stock | Current Stock minus Reserved Stock.                         |
| Reorder Level   | Stock threshold at which replenishment should be initiated. |

---

# Data Warehouse Terms

| Term             | Definition                                                                           |
|------------------|--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| Fact Table       | Table containing measurable business events, such as sales transactions.             |
| Dimension Table  | Table containing descriptive attributes used for filtering and analysis.             |
| Star Schema      | A dimensional model consisting of fact and dimension tables optimized for analytics. |
| Surrogate Key    | System-generated integer key used within the data warehouse.                         |
| Business Key     | Natural identifier originating from operational source systems.                      |
| SCD Type 2       | A technique for preserving historical changes by creating new dimension records.     |
| High Watermark   | The highest processed value used for incremental ETL loading.                        |
| Incremental Load | ETL process that loads only new or modified records.                                 |

---

# Reporting KPIs

| KPI                   | Definition                                                                 |
|-----------------------|----------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| Total Revenue         | Sum of all Sales Amount values.                                            |
| Total Profit          | Sum of all Profit Amount values.                                           |
| Total Transactions    | Count of completed sales transactions.                                     |
| Top Selling Product   | Product with the highest sales quantity or revenue.                        |
| Best Performing Store | Store with the highest revenue or profit.                                  |
| Inventory Turnover    | Measure of how frequently inventory is sold and replenished over a period. |

---

# Summary

The Business Glossary provides standardized definitions for the business entities, metrics, and data warehouse concepts used throughout the project. Maintaining consistent terminology improves communication, reporting accuracy, and collaboration between business and technical teams.
