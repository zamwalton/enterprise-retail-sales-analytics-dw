# Purpose

This document identifies the business and technical stakeholders involved in the Enterprise Retail Sales Analytics Data Warehouse project. It defines their responsibilities and expected contributions throughout the project lifecycle.

---

# Stakeholder Matrix

| Stakeholder                  | Department            | Responsibility                                                |
|------------------------------|-----------------------|---------------------------------------------------------------|
| Executive Sponsor            | Executive Management  | Approves project funding, priorities, and business objectives |
| Retail Operations Manager    | Retail Operations     | Provides POS and store business requirements                  |
| E-Commerce Manager           | Digital Commerce      | Provides online sales requirements                            |
| Marketing Manager            | Marketing             | Owns promotions and campaign data                             |
| HR Manager                   | Human Resources       | Owns employee data                                            |
| Procurement Manager          | Procurement           | Owns supplier information                                     |
| Warehouse Manager            | Inventory Operations  | Owns inventory data                                           |
| Data Engineer                | Data Engineering      | Designs and develops ETL pipelines and data warehouse         |
| Database Administrator (DBA) | Database Team         | Manages PostgreSQL performance, backups, and security         |
| BI Developer                 | Business Intelligence | Develops Power BI dashboards and reporting models             |
| Business Analyst             | Business Analysis     | Defines reporting requirements and validates business rules   |
| End Users                    | Business Teams        | Consume dashboards and analytical reports                     |

---

# Stakeholder Responsibilities

## Executive Sponsor

- Approves project objectives
- Reviews project progress
- Validates business outcomes

---

## Business Analyst

- Collects business requirements
- Defines reporting metrics
- Reviews business rules
- Validates delivered functionality

---

## Data Engineer

Responsible for:

- ETL Pipeline
- Incremental Loading
- SCD Type 2
- Data Validation
- Logging Framework
- Data Warehouse Development

---

## Database Administrator

Responsible for:

- PostgreSQL installation
- Database performance
- Index management
- Backup and recovery

---

## BI Developer

Responsible for:

- Power BI datasets
- Dashboard development
- KPI calculations
- Report optimization
